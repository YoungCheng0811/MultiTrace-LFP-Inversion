# Data availability

The Marmousi benchmark model and the Netherlands F3 Block dataset are publicly
available from their corresponding repositories. The source code,
configuration files, split indices, and a lightweight synthetic demonstration
associated with this study are available in this repository. The original
third-party datasets are not redistributed and should be obtained from their
official sources in accordance with the applicable terms of use.

The included arrays in `examples/` are newly generated synthetic interface
examples. They are not Marmousi or F3 data and cannot reproduce the numerical
results reported in the manuscript.
